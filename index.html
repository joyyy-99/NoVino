Our Story

Born from the vibrant post-Detty December celebrations in Nairobi, NoVino was created to fill the gap for premium non-alcoholic alternatives during Dry January. Founded by three friends who wanted to continue the social joy of gathering without alcohol, we craft our drinks using traditional Kenyan ingredients like hibiscus, baobab, and tamarind.

Our mission is to provide sophisticated, flavorful alternatives that celebrate Kenyan heritage while supporting mindful drinking choices. Each bottle contains carefully sourced ingredients from local farmers, supporting sustainable agriculture across Kenya.
